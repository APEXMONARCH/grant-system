Features

Everything You Need to Run a Grant Program

Built for institutions that need transparency, speed, and control over research funding workflows.

Kanban Review Board

Move applications through stages — Submitted, Under Review, Approved, or Rejected — with a visual drag-and-drop workflow.

Multi-Step Applications

Applicants complete guided forms and upload supporting documents (PDF, DOC, images) before submission.

Fund Analytics

Admins see live fund statistics, Chart.js visualisations, and date-range reports at a glance.

Beneficiary Tracking

Track approved recipients, monitor disbursements, and export beneficiary data to CSV.

In-App Messaging

Communicate between admins and applicants without leaving the platform.

User Management

Role-based access control for admins, reviewers, and applicants with secure JWT authentication.
